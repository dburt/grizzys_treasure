🍯 Grizzy's Treasure 3D

You're a lemming. Sneak across the meadow, steal the Yummy jar off the plinth, and carry it back over the white line.

Grizzy patrols with a vision cone — if he sees you moving, you're bounced home. If he spots you carrying the jar, the chase is on and the saucepan comes out!

The Yummy's locked inside the ranger's hut with Grizzy. Slip in through the doorway on your side, use the crates, barrels and rocks to break his line of sight, then sneak back out.
